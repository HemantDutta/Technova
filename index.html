About Technova

Technova publication is a global academic publisher of journals multiple disciplines with broad scope. It serve free and open access journals to all Industry, academicians, consultants, scientists, researchers, scholars and students.

Technova Publications publish information and technological solutions that enhance the performance of science, health, and technology professionals. All articles which are published by Technova publications have undergone peer review and upon acceptance are immediately and permanently free for everyone to read and download.

About Us

Technova Publications is an open access scientific publisher started with the aim of dissemination of science and research worldwide and available freely for readers without any cost. We follow the ethical policies and standards for publishing the research worldwide. We follow the quality review process and process the articles as per the open access standard peer review rules and regulations. We publish original research articles, survey articles, review articles, case studies and book reviews in all areas of Science and Technology.
